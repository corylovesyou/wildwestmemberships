The Opportunity

Your models are the brand.
Their wardrobe should work as hard as they do.

What Your Agency Needs

  • Test Shoots & Portfolio Builds Fresh looks for every model's book, pulled, shot, and returned without buying a single piece.
  • Editorial & Campaign Pulls High-fashion editorial looks across multiple stories, kept for the full shoot window.
  • Red Carpets & Events Luxury ready-to-wear for high-profile appearances, premieres, and industry events.
  • Everyday Luxury Ready-to-Wear An extension of your models' closets, on-call access to premium wardrobe at all times.

The Shift WWSH Creates

  • Less Wardrobe Spend Discounted rental access replaces expensive outright purchases for every shoot and event.
  • More Budget for Bookings Redirect styling spend toward casting, production, and model development.
  • An Extension of the Closet WWSH becomes your agency's permanent on-call wardrobe partner for every model.
  • Instant Access, Zero Storage No buying, storing, or maintaining wardrobe. Pull what you need and return when done.

Corporate membership gives your agency dedicated, discounted access across every shoot, pull, and public appearance.

Joinders

Pre-approved member access,
included on every corporate account.

A joinder is a person approved by Wild West Social House to use the corporate account for a specific occasion. They are not permanent account members, they are individually approved by WWSH at least 48 hours before they can access the account.

2

Per Account, Per Year

Every corporate membership, regardless of tier, includes 2 joinders per year. These do not roll over to the following year.

48h

WWSH Approval Required

The joinder's name and intended date of use must be submitted to WWSH at least 48 hours in advance.

✓

Right to Approve or Decline

Wild West Social House reserves the right to approve or decline any joinder at their discretion, at any time.

Joinders must be individually approved by WWSH no less than 48 hours before their date of use. 2 per corporate account per year. Non-transferable.
